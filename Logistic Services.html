ABOUT US

Dear Customer, welcome to the fam :)

The Coffee Cup, a part of Coffee Cup Global Limited, is India’s favourite hangout for coffee and conversations. Popularly known as TCC, we strive to provide the best experience to our guests. Our coffees are sourced from thousands of small coffee planters, who made us who we are today and we're glad to be a part of their lives. We opened our first cafe in 2003 at jublihills in Hyderabad – the youth and the young at heart immediately took to the cafe, and it continues to be one of the most happening places in the city.TCC to the youth is a “hangout” spot where they meet people, make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee.It's been an exciting journey since then to becoming the largest organised retail cafe chain in the country.

FOUNDER'S STORY

B V S Manoj is the Founder & CEO of The Coffee Cup. He is ardently passionate about coffee, bringing the best of Indian coffees to the Indians. With 9 years of international experience across 5 countries in energy industry at Schlumberger, he's earned accolades like the 2021 International Young Professional Award. Armed with diverse qualifications, including a bachelor's degree from IIT Dhanbad, a Diploma in law from NUJS Kolkata, and a Master’s in Political Marketing from Rome Business School Italy, Manoj aims to democratize specialty coffee in India through his company, The Coffee Cup.
